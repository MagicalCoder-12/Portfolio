Introduction

Shadow Avengers is developed by AJI Creators. This Privacy Policy explains how we and our third-party partners collect, use, and share information when you use our mobile application.

By using the Game, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Compliance Notice

Shadow Avengers complies with:

  • Google Play Developer Policies
  • GDPR (for EU users)
  • CCPA (for California residents)
  • COPPA (we do not knowingly collect data from children under 13)

Our ads implementation follows Google's Families Policy requirements.

Information Collection & Use

Gameplay Data

Shadow Avengers stores the following data only on your device:

  • Game progress and level completion
  • High scores and achievements
  • Settings preferences

This data is stored in local files (high_score.dat, game_progress.dat) and is never transmitted to our servers.

Advertising Analytics

We use Google AdMob for ads. AdMob may collect:

  • Device information (model, OS version, IP address)
  • Non-personal identifiers (Advertising ID)
  • Ad interactions (clicks, impressions)
  • Usage data for analytics and fraud prevention

Data is encrypted using TLS. See Google's Privacy Policy.

Personalized Ads

We may serve personalized ads via AdMob. If you're in the EEA/UK, we use Google UMP to ask for consent first. You can opt out:

  • In the Game's settings (if available)
  • Through Google's Ad Settings
  • Via your device's ad tracking options

Non-personalized ads may still be shown based on contextual information.

Children's Privacy

This game is intended for a general audience and does not target children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

If you believe we might have any information from or about a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

Data Retention

Since Shadow Avengers does not transmit personal data to our servers, we do not retain any user information.

Advertising partners retain data according to their respective privacy policies.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights including:

  • Access: Request copies of your data
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out: Object to processing of your data

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information below.

Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information:

  • All ad-related data transmission uses TLS encryption
  • Game data remains locally stored on your device
  • Regular reviews of our data practices

However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new policy on this page
  • Updating the "Effective Date" at the top
  • In-app notification for significant changes

You're advised to review this policy periodically for any changes.
