Privacy Policy

MPH Songs - Song Lyrics Manager App

This Privacy Policy describes how MPH Songs ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application MPH Songs (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

When you use our App, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Song Data: We store song lyrics, titles, language information, and song categories (such as Choir or Good Friday songs) locally on your device to provide offline access.
  • Device Information: We may collect information about your device, including device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers.
  • Network Information: The App requires internet connection to sync song data from our servers. We do not track or store your browsing history or network activity.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our App
  • Sync song data between our servers and your device
  • Store your song preferences locally on your device
  • Ensure proper functioning of the App's offline capabilities

3. Data Storage

All song data is stored locally on your device using Room database. This data remains on your device until you:

  • Clear the app's data through your device settings
  • Uninstall the application

We do not store any personal information on external servers.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to third parties. The only data transmission occurs between the App and our servers for the purpose of:

  • Downloading song lyrics and metadata
  • Checking for app updates

5. Internet Permission

The App requires internet permission to:

  • Fetch the latest song lyrics from our servers
  • Sync choir and Good Friday song categories
  • Ensure content is up to date

The App functions offline using cached data when no internet connection is available.

6. Children's Privacy

Our App does not collect any personal information from children or any users. The App is designed to provide song lyrics for personal and religious use.

7. Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect the data transmitted between the App and our servers. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

8. Third-Party Services

Our App does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the App and updating the "Effective Date" at the bottom of this page.

10. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Clear all locally stored data by clearing app data in your device settings
  • Uninstall the application to remove all associated data
  • Contact us with any questions about this Privacy Policy
