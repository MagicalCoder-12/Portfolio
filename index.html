Shadow Avengers - Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 1, 2025

Introduction

Shadow Avengers ("the Game") is developed by AJI Creators. This Privacy Policy explains how we and our third-party partners collect, use, and share information when you use the Game.

Information We Collect

1. Data Collected by the Game

The game does not collect personal information. It stores non-personal data (like high scores, level progress) locally on your device in files like high_score.dat and game_progress.dat. We don't see or share this data.

2. Data Collected by AdMob

We use Google AdMob for ads. AdMob may collect:

  • Device Info: IP address, device ID (e.g., Advertising ID)
  • Ad Interactions: Clicks, impressions
  • Usage: For analytics, ad personalization, and fraud prevention

Data is encrypted using TLS. See Google’s Privacy Policy.

Personalized Ads

We may serve personalized ads via AdMob. If you're in the EEA/UK, we use Google UMP to ask for consent first. You can opt out:

  • In the Game’s settings (if available)
  • Google’s Ad Settings
  • Through your device’s ad tracking options

Children’s Privacy

This game is intended for a general audience and does not target children under 13. We do not enable COPPA tags since it's not a kids app.

Your Rights

Under laws like GDPR or CCPA, you may:

  • Request access, correction, or deletion of data
  • Opt out of personalized ads

Contact: royaloverlord.aicreator@gmail.com

Security

AdMob handles its own security using TLS encryption. We do not transmit or collect personal data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this page as needed. You’ll be notified via the Game or this site if any major changes are made.
